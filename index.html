Easily organize your job searching

With our platform, you forward any email related to your job search, we'll scan it and give you an organized overview of where you are in the process of applying to each one of your jobs.

Here's how it works

1. You receieve an email from a confirmation email after applying to a job

This email can be from any company you are applying to. There's no set list!

2. You forward that email to our private server

3. We scan the email and update your job tracking list to reflect where you are in the application process

4. We delete the email you forwarded to us

We have a privacy promise where we do not keep any of the emails you send us. We only use them to extract relevant information related to your job search to show you. You can delete any data at any time, and we do not sell it.
